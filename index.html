Go Fish is a premiere five star restaurant, part of Clearwater's Sand Castle Resort attractions. It claims to be Tampa Bay's first all-glass undersea restaurant. The domed walls and roof offer a constant background of marine animals and coral scenery that can be enjoyed alongside lunch and dinner, all served 16 feet below sea level. Go Fish can be reserved for special occasions, events or simply enjoy a private dinner with family. Children are welcome to explore a specially designed for them play area.
